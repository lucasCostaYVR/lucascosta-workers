## 4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information.

### Your Consent
When you first visit our website, we ask for your consent to use cookies.
- **If you accept:** We place a persistent cookie (`lc_anon_id`) to recognize you across visits and provide a better experience.
- **If you deny:** We do not store any persistent identifiers. We may still collect anonymous, transient data for that specific session to ensure the website functions correctly, but this data is not linked to a persistent profile.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or using the "Reset Consent" feature on our website.
